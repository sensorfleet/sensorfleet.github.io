About us

Our team has a long experience in developing cyber security sensor technology for the national early warning system protecting the critical infrastructure in Finland. At the inception of SensorFleet in 2018, we set out to re-imagine cyber security monitoring.

Our core principles

We remain committed to continuous improvement and evolution of our solution's features.

Data privacy

End users will always have full control of the data Sensors collect and the solution is deployable fully on premise. No data or metadata will be shared with us without the explicit arrangement where SensorFleet hosts the solution, or parts of it.

Security of our own solution

Coming from the security testing background, security of our solution is a priority for us. Layered architecture, least privileges principle, controlled network access, data encryption both in rest and transit and configurable data retention are among the controls we use.
